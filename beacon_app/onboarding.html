Beacon How it works
For people without a safety net

Beacon is built for the unconnected.

Not everyone has family to call or a friend nearby. Beacon is a quiet backup: a way to be seen and protected when you feel completely alone.

Guardian Angels

Some people choose to watch over others.

Guardian Angels are volunteers who respond to Beacons, check in on you, and can be sponsored or tipped by the community for the care they give.

Emergency Beacon

In danger, you press one button.

When you trigger a Beacon, your location and emergency info can be shared with people you trust and nearby responders. Beacon never replaces 911 — it backs you up.

You control what you share and who sees you. Let’s set up your profile next.
